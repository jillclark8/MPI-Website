Who We Are

Colorado Roots.
National Strength.

Metro Pavers has been a fixture on the Colorado Front Range since 1975. Five decades of relationships, reputation, and results — built one project at a time.

Now operating as MPI, A Sunland Company, we've added something no regional paving contractor in Denver can match: the financial strength, bonding capacity, and operational power of one of the nation's largest asphalt enterprises. What that means for our clients is simple — bigger capacity, better pricing, and a partner who will be here for the next 50 years.

Backed by Sunland Asphalt

Sunland is one of the most respected names in commercial paving across the American Southwest and Rocky Mountain region. Our partnership gives MPI clients access to preferred material pricing, expanded bonding on large municipal and commercial contracts, and national-scale resources delivered with local Front Range knowledge.
